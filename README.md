Beef-Cakes
==========

A recipe for a delicious beefy snack.

Ingredients
----------

- 1 pound 70%+ Lean Ground Beef
- 1 package French and Onion Soup mix
- 1 Diced Onion
- 3 Slices of Bread cubed and left out Over Night
- 2 Eggs
- 1/2 pound Bacon Strips
- 1 container Sour Cream
- Chives
